Data practices

Privacy & Tracking Transparency

Understand how we handle affiliate tracking, analytics, and cookies while staying aligned with Amazon’s policies.

We respect your privacy and operate SSDeal in compliance with the Amazon Associates Program Policies and applicable regulations.

Affiliate Tracking

Links that point to Amazon are tagged with our Associate ID so Amazon can attribute qualifying purchases. Amazon may place cookies or use other tracking technologies to measure referrals and fulfill the Associates Program. You can learn more in Amazon’s Privacy Notice.

Information We Collect

  • Site analytics: We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand visit counts, page popularity, and device types. Data is aggregated and does not include names, emails, or precise locations.
  • Emails you send us: When you email hello@ssdeal.example we store the message solely to respond.

What We Don’t Collect

We do not collect payment data, social security numbers, or shopping cart details; checkout takes place on Amazon or other retailers. We do not sell or rent any information to third parties.

Cookies & Control

Amazon and other retailers may set cookies to track referrals as required by the Associates Program. You can clear or block cookies using your browser settings. Rejecting cookies may limit our ability to receive commission but will not affect your purchase price.

Children’s Privacy

SSDeal is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children; if you believe we have, email privacy@ssdeal.example so we can delete it.

Policy Updates

We review this page whenever Amazon updates its Program Policies (last updated October 15, 2025). Any material change will be dated at the top of the page.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email privacy@ssdeal.example or use our contact page.
